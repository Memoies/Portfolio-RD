Core Values

Cultural Preservation
By preserving heritage and promoting culture, the branding project aim to enhance community values in all activities. We are committed to keeping the city's past and future intertwined.

Modernization
We pledge to adopt new technologies and innovative approaches to improve infrastructure, services, and experiences for both residents and tourists.

Environmental Protection
Once people come to embrace the new look and identity of their city - Vũng Tàu, awareness will be heightened. Both locals and tourists will contribute to preserving Vũng Tàu as a clean and green city, showing respect for the environment, encompassing the sea, coastal areas, and natural landscapes.

Mission and Vision

Mission
Promote diversification, sustainable development, and create a friendly environment for both local residents and tourists. Vũng Tàu is fostering culture and community values in all city activities.

Vision
Becoming an appealing, diverse, and exciting destination where everyone is welcomed, cultural and natural diversity is respected, and continuous development and progress thrive.

from IDEAS

Simplicity
The brand identity is designed with simplicity at its core, featuring a modern and concise logo. This makes the brand easily recognizable and friendly to all, especially appealing to a younger audience.

Sustainability
Using only strokes (lines) in the brand identity adds flexibility, as strokes can be customized and color-changed easily to suit various purposes and different contexts.

Maintaining Core Elements
Despite changes, ensuring that core elements, such as the city's familiar icon, remain intact to establish continuous recognition and a direct connection to Vũng Tàu.
